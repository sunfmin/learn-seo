# Mission: SEO, AEO & Marketing Automation (for builders)

## Why
Felix is a strong developer (The Plant) who is new to SEO and wants to **build software that does SEO/AEO marketing automatically** — content generation, site audits, rank/citation tracking, structured-data pipelines. He needs the domain deep enough to encode it into tools, then apply that machine to his own product, client sites, and content.

## Success looks like
- Can explain the crawl → index → rank pipeline (classic search) AND the retrieve → generate → cite pipeline (AI answer engines) well enough to design tooling against each.
- Can write a script/agent that audits a site for the technical SEO + AEO signals that actually move the needle (and justify each check with a primary source).
- Can build a content pipeline that produces pages structured to rank in Google AND get cited by ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity/Google AI Overviews.
- Can measure results: track rankings, impressions, and AI-citation share over time via APIs.

## Constraints
- Developer mindset — prefers precise mechanisms, APIs, and code over hand-wavy "best practices."
- Time-boxed: short lessons, one tangible win each.
- Wants automation-first: every tactic learned should map to "how do I make software do this."

## Out of scope (for now)
- Paid ads / PPC, social-media marketing, email marketing — focus is organic search + AI answer engines.
- Deep copywriting craft (beyond what's needed for ranking/citation).
